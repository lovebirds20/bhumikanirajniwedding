Events

19th December

Ganesh Pooja and Santek 10AM - 12PM

Ganesh Pooja and Santek are traditional Indian pre-wedding rituals. Ganesh Pooja is performed to seek the blessings of Lord Ganesha for a prosperous and obstacle-free wedding. Santek is a cleansing ceremony symbolizing the spiritual and physical preparation of the bride and groom for their new journey.

Sangeet 7PM - 12AM

Sangeet is a joyous pre-wedding celebration filled with music, dance, and festivities. It brings together family and friends of the bride and groom to celebrate their union with vibrant performances, laughter, and bonding.

20th December

Haldi 10AM - 11AM

Haldi is a sacred pre-wedding ritual where a turmeric paste is applied to the bride and groom. It symbolizes purification, protection, and blessing them with a radiant glow as they prepare for their big day.

Wedding 4PM - 10PM

This is the main event in the entire wedding when the bride and the groom tie the knot. The groom comes with the barat and is greeted by the bride side with some dhol of course as maximum number of guests would be attending this event. This is when the couple circle the sacred fire and are finally declared husband and wife.
